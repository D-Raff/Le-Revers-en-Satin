Who Are We?

Le Revers en Satin (The Satin Lapel), is a business dedicated to creating bespoke suits, clothing items and accessories for the modern man who loves the classic look and feel derived from the fashion of the 17th century businessman, all the way to the 19th century gentleman.

We are dedicated to excellence and the pursuit of perfection, sourcing the finest quality materials for our products and putting them in the capable hands of our staff; who work tirelessly to create the highest quality garments with the perfect proportions to suit the client.

All of our suits and clothing items are Bespoke, meaning we create almost everything from scratch, all to the clients demands. We also offer alterations for your loved clothing items.

We accept gifting packgage requests, with as many or as little items as you want. create a personalized gift package for a loved one or good friend and give them a gift they will always remember.

Confused as to what you should match together? Don't be! Our in-house Stylists and advisors are always ready and willing to help and guide your choices as to what to wear and how to wear them.
